        How to play

        • Each day brings a real recording and its real engraved score — with the composer's name inked out.
        • Each of your six tries must name both the composer (surname is enough) and the piece, together.
        • A hint (year, era, genre) costs a try. Wrong composers are struck from the list.
        • Getting the piece right at any point doubles your points — and we'll tell you if you've at least got the right genre.
        • Fewer tries = more points. Harder tiers multiply them (×2, ×3). Dailies build your career score and streak — one attempt per day, so make it count.
        • Encores and challenges are for glory, not points.
