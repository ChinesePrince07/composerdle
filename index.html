        How to play

        • Each day, one composer hides behind six fun-fact clues, cryptic first, obvious last.
        • You have six tries: guess (surname is enough) or ask for the next clue — either spends a try.
        • Wrong guesses are struck off the list.
        • By Facts is endless practice — a new random composer each round, with clues that reshuffle every time.
        • Pick a level: Easy serves common-knowledge clues, Hard only the niche, obscure ones (the famous-work giveaways never appear).
        • Every win here scores too. Mode II · By Ear adds real recordings, real scores, and the daily streak puzzle.

        My stats

        Welcome to Composerdle

        • Six tries to name the mystery composer — surname is enough. Hints and extra clues each cost a try.
        • By Facts is endless practice: six fun-fact clues, cryptic first, obvious last.
        • By Ear — a real recording and its engraved score, name inked out. Naming the piece too is optional — right = points doubled.
        • Scoring: fewer tries = more points, Medium ×2, Hard ×3. Every win counts toward your career; the daily puzzle also builds your streak.

        Every performer needs a stage name — it is how your scores appear on the leaderboard.
